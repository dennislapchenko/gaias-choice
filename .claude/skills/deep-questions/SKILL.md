# Deep questions — deepening the owners' persona-identity

The site's only asset is trust, and trust comes from a real worldview underneath
the words. This practice keeps that worldview *alive* — not a static bio, but a
soul that keeps revealing itself. The owners want to be asked genuinely deep,
touching, many-layered questions, and want every answer preserved verbatim.

## Where things live

- **The portrait it feeds:** `context/persona-context.md` — Lida's voice + Denis's
  worldview (his seven pillars). Read it before composing questions.
- **The worldview steward:** the **`gaia-mentor`** skill carries the six threads,
  the teachers, and the mission this all serves — invoke it alongside this one
  when the questions touch mission/positioning, and to keep questions in-worldview.
- **The influence map:** `context/ideology-context.md` — the teachers (Chek,

  `/Users/dlapcenko/dev/dlapcenko/garden-of-atlantis` (*Garden of Atlantis*) —
  already read in full and distilled into `persona-context.md`; do **NOT**
  re-read it when composing questions. The owner will say when the garden has
  new material worth a fresh pass (then: skip business/food-business plans;
  mine the metaphysics, meanings, quests, and blank/`WIP` headings).

## Non-negotiables (this is the whole point)

1. **No scientific reductionism. Ever.** Their worldview is anti-reductionist by
   design. Honor — do not debunk, hedge, or "well, actually" — invisible energies,
   astrology (the real ephemeris), biodynamics, ancient/lost-civilization
   knowledge, and origin views **including that humanity was seeded/created by
   non-human intelligences (the Anunnaki account, which Denis finds most
   plausible)**. Treat these as live, respected premises. Be *inspired* by them.
2. **Verbatim record, always.** Answers are truth-first source material. Log them
   untouched (typos, emoji, caps, all of it) in `persona-context.md` → *Appendix B
   — Deep-questions log*: Denis's under his dated heading, Lida's under the `#LIDA`
   marker. Then fold *distilled* insight up into the worldview/voice sections and
   add any newly named teacher to `ideology-context.md` too.
3. **Reverence, not a quiz.** These are not trivia or a survey with options. Warm,
   sincere, first-person, often reaching for the places their own writing goes
   quiet. Never patronize; never perform.

## How to compose a good one (the method that landed)

- **Ground it in their actual words** — the garden, the persona file, a review or
  Journal entry they just wrote. A great question shows you *read* them.
- **Find the silences.** Their most honest material is where the writing stops: a
  blank `## What is Life and what is Death?` heading, a `WIP`, a "just planting
  the seeds now." Ask into the gap.
- **Layer it.** Open with the premise, turn it once, then land a concrete personal
  edge (a tension they live, not an abstraction). One question, several angles.
- **Make it experiential (Ralston).** Denis prizes direct knowing over belief —
  ask what they've *lived*, not what they'd *argue*.
- **Hold the tension, don't resolve it.** The van's search for anchor vs. the
  permaculturist's rooted system; the Level-42 engineer building the "silicon
  overlords" he's wary of. Name the paradox; let them answer it.

## Always send in both languages

**Every deep question goes out in English *and* Russian**, both versions
together — the on-request set, the single closing question, and the future
Telegram delivery alike. The ask reaches Denis; he replies, then forwards the
Russian version to Lida so she answers too. So each question needs a faithful
RU rendering (not a machine calque — match the warmth and register; consult
`context/persona-context.md` and `content/locales/README.md`'s glossary).
Record each person's answer verbatim under their own heading (Denis dated,
Lida `#LIDA`) per non-negotiable 2.

## The rhythm

- **On request:** a set of ~5, prose (not the option-picker tool — these are
  open). Then record + fold as above.
- **Periodic, at the end of a content-working session** (manage-site loops): drop
  **ONE** question as a warm closing line. Optional and non-naggy — skip it if the
  session was heavy or purely mechanical. If they answer, record it verbatim.
- **Future (backlog, not built):** auto-deliver one question every other day to
