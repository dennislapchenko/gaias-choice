After seven months on the road, our "essentials" list got a lot shorter — and a
lot more deliberate. Here's the natural, low-waste gear that earned a permanent
spot in the bag — on any kind of trip.

1. **GOTS organic cotton swaddles** — swaddle, blanket, sunshade, all in one.
2. **Wool sleep sack** — temperature-regulating for cold nights, no synthetics.
3. **Stainless steel bento** — plastic-free first foods that survive drops.
4. **Glass baby bottles with silicone sleeves** — no plastic, easy to sterilize.
5. **Beeswax food wraps** — retired our cling film for good.
6. **Fragrance-free castile soap** — one bottle for bodies, dishes, and floors.
7. **Organic cotton washcloths** — reusable wipes that rinse and line-dry fast.
8. **Bamboo dish brush** — compostable head, no plastic bristles shedding.
9. **Stainless collapsible cup** — packs flat, no BPA, endless uses.
10. **Wool dryer balls** — soften laundry with zero fragrance or single-use sheets.

## How to use this list

Don't buy all ten at once. Start with whatever you're currently solving with
plastic or fragrance, replace it well, and move down the list. Buying once and
buying right is the whole point.
