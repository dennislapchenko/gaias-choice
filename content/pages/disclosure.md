title: Affiliate disclosure
---

Some links on Gaia's Choice are **affiliate links**. If you click one and make
a purchase, we may earn a small commission. **You never pay more** — the
commission comes out of the seller's side, not your price.

Here is exactly how we handle it:

- **We only link to products we have actually bought and used.** The review
  always comes first; any affiliate relationship comes second, if at all.
- **Commissions never change a verdict.** Our reviews name flaws, and we
  recommend cheaper or non-affiliated alternatives whenever they're genuinely
  better — you'll find products we link with no commission attached at all.
- **Free review units are disclosed** in the review itself, and brands never
  get approval over what we write.
- **Sponsored content, if we ever run it, will be clearly labeled** at the top
  of the page. As of today, there is none.

Affiliate income is how a site like this stays independent — no paywalls, no
