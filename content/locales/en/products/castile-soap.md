When you're traveling, every product you carry is weight and space. A single
soap that does five jobs is the definition of packing smart.

Unscented **plant-oil castile soap** is our one-bottle wonder: diluted, it's baby
wash, hand soap, dish soap, and floor cleaner. Concentrated, a little goes a
long way, so one bottle lasts far longer than it looks.

## Why we love it

- **Ingredients** — plant oils, nothing synthetic, and genuinely fragrance-free
  (not "unscented" with masking perfume).
- **Multi-use** — one bottle replaces a shelf of single-purpose cleaners.
- **Gentle** — mild enough for a newborn, effective enough for greasy dishes.

## How to use it

It's concentrated — always dilute. We keep a small pump bottle of a weak dilution
for hands and a separate mix for dishes. Undiluted it's too strong for skin.
