If we were only allowed to keep one baby item in the bag, this would
probably be it.

A single **GOTS-certified organic cotton muslin swaddle** does the job of
five items at once: swaddle, light blanket, sun shade over the car seat,
nursing cover, and emergency picnic blanket. Where every item has to earn
its place, that kind of versatility settles the matter.

## Why it won us over

- **Fabric** — 100% organic cotton, no synthetic blends and no scented finishes.
- **Breathable fabric** — the open muslin weave matters most in a warm car, tent, or camper van.
- **Washing and drying** — it softens with every wash and dries quickly on a line strung between two doors.

## What you need to know

The weave is deliberately loose, so this is a warm-season item, not a
winter one — don't rely on it as your only layer in the cold. On chilly
nights we pair it with a wool sleep sack (reviewed separately).

> Buy two. One is always in the wash while the other is doing duty as a
> sun shade.
