If we could only keep one baby item in the bag, it might be this.

A single **GOTS-certified organic cotton** muslin does the work of five things:
swaddle, light blanket, sun shade over the car seat, nursing cover, and an
emergency picnic layer. In a space where everything has to earn its keep, that
kind of versatility is everything.

## Why it made the cut

- **Fabric** — 100% organic cotton, no synthetic blends, no fragrance finish.
- **Breathability** — the open muslin weave matters a lot in a warm car, tent, or camper.
- **Wash & dry** — gets softer each wash and dries fast on a line between two doors.

## What to know

The weave is deliberately loose, so it's a warm-season layer, not a winter one.
For cold nights we pair it with a wool sleep sack (reviewed separately).

> Buy two. One is always in the wash, and the second becomes the sunshade.
