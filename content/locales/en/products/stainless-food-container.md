Plastic food containers crack, stain, and hold onto smells — three things you
really notice when your kitchen is the size of a shoebox.

This **18/8 stainless steel** bento has been our daily driver for snacks and
first foods. No plastic touching the food, no lingering odor, and it shrugs off
the drops that end plastic tubs.

## The good

- **Material** — food-grade stainless, silicone gasket, no BPA/BPS anywhere.
- **Durability** — dented once, never cracked. It will outlast the trip.
- **Cleaning** — wipes clean with no staining from tomato or turmeric.

## The trade-off

The lid is only **leak-resistant**, not fully leak-proof — fine for solids and
thick purees, but we don't pack it with soup. That's the one point it loses.

For anything liquid we reach for a small glass jar instead.
