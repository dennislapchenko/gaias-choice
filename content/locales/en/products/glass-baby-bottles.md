Plastic bottles cloud, scratch, and start to smell after enough washes. Glass
just… doesn't. For something your baby's mouth is on every day, that mattered to us.

These **borosilicate glass** bottles sterilize cleanly and never take on odor.
The wrap-around **silicone sleeve** is what makes them travel-friendly — grippy
for small hands and a real buffer against knocks.

## The good

- **Material** — glass and medical-grade silicone, no plastic on the feed path.
- **Hygiene** — no clouding or staining, easy to see they're actually clean.
- **Grip** — the sleeve turns a slippery bottle into a droppable one that survives.

## The trade-off

Glass is heavier than plastic, and even sleeved it isn't drop-proof onto stone.
It loses a point there — but for daily use it's the one we reach for.

For long hikes we decant into a lighter stainless cup instead.
