**Verdict:** answer in one or two sentences — for the reader who'll leave in
ten seconds. Would we buy it again — knowing what we know now? Did this sofa
become a place the dog goes to on its own, or did it stay just an empty
structure in the corner of the camper?

## Why we bought it

Nobody wakes up wanting to read a review about a dog sofa — they wake up
because the dog has climbed onto the human bed again or is whining on the cold
floor of the camper. Name that real problem: where the dog used to sleep, what
wasn't working for us — fur on the bed, cold from the floor, cramped space —
and which options (a basket, a mat, this sofa) we were choosing between.

## How this item lived with us

How much time the dog actually spends using it, and under what conditions —
in the camper while driving, parked, in the heat and in the damp. Did we buy
it ourselves or was it a gift (write honestly about a gift right here). Did
the dog chew the upholstery, did moisture leak inside, did we have to carry
the sofa differently than intended — and what would we truly miss if it were
gone tomorrow.

## What's actually good

Specifics instead of adjectives: how much it weighs and how much space it
takes up in the camper, how easy the cover is to remove and wash, how quickly
it dries after a puddle or a bath, whether it holds its shape after months of
the dog's weight — not "very comfortable." Give that one moment when the dog
chose this sofa over its old spot on its own.

## What we'd change

Every review has a downside — if there's no other one, the downside is the
price, a size that doesn't fit into the camper the way we wanted, or which
dogs (by weight, breed, age) the sofa doesn't suit. And then the honest one
that a paid reviewer never writes: **what would make us take this
recommendation back?** Did the dog tear the upholstery within a month, did the
filling start clumping, did the cover get so damp that the smell won't wash
out?

## Who this is for

And which dogs or which campers should better pass on it — one honest line
each: too large a dog, too cramped a van, too hot a climate for this material.
And then the reverse question worth asking about everything you bring along:
**how long can the dog and its owners comfortably do without this sofa?** If
the item isn't for you — let the money stay with you: that's what this site
exists for.
