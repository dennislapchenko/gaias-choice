## What we set out to do

Why Lithuania was even on the route, and how "pandas" entered the plan at
all — did you know they were there, or was it a rumor, a sign on the road, a
tip from someone you met? Say what you expected to find before you found it.

## What actually happened

The real version of seeing them. The place, the hour, what the enclosure or
the room actually looked like, the first thing that struck you as strange or
wonderful about pandas turning up here of all places. If the kids' reaction
rewrote the moment, say so plainly.

## The hard part

Was it the drive to get there, the language, the crowd, a closed gate, a
tired toddler at the worst possible minute? Name the friction honestly —
that's what makes the discovery itself land harder.

## What worked (or what we'd change)

How you actually got to see them, or what you'd do differently if you were
telling another family how to find pandas in Lithuania. One honest lesson is
enough — "we still don't fully understand why they're here" counts.

## One thing worth keeping

A detail, a photo you'll keep, a phrase one of the kids said, a tip for
anyone chasing the same strange discovery — leave the reader something to
take with them, the way you'd send a friend home with a jar.
