---
title: Support us
---

Everything here is free — the reviews, the Compass courses, the almanac — and we
mean to keep it that way: no paywalls, no ad clutter, no selling your data. If
something we wrote saved you a bad purchase or a long night, and you'd like to
chip in, we'd be quietly delighted. It's never expected, and the site stays
exactly the same either way.
