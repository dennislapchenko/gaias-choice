# Course plan — «Честный сайт с нуля» / "An honest site from zero" (tag: founder-guide)

**Status:** all 5 chapters shipped 2026-07-05 — a retrofit of the original
founder guides into course form (same slugs, same facts, continuity weave
added). Chapters get *retold from lived experience* as roadmap milestones
land; that retirement is the course's own chapter-1 promise, so don't "finish"
it by other means.

**What this is.** The five founder guides reframed as a Compass epic — a
**deliberate 5-chapter deviation** from the 11-chapter blueprint in
[epic-writing-context.md](epic-writing-context.md): Gate = ch. 1, Path =
ch. 2–4, Summit = ch. 5. Everything else follows the blueprint: Trail block,
named concepts, callbacks, one seed per Path chapter, Bridge (Практика /
Теперь вы умеете / Проверьте себя / hook). The one-line **founder-note
blockquote stays directly under each Trail block** — it's a truth device
(these are internal playbooks, published):

> **Гайд для основателей.** Написан для нас самих, пока мы поднимаем сайт с
> нуля, — честные заметки, изданные как курс.

**Spine artifact:** a **launched site** — ours (the one being read) and the
reader's own if they build along. Ch. 1 starts it (promise on paper, weekly
rhythm in the calendar, raw-notes file for the first product); every chapter's
Практика adds a piece (claims check + verdict draft; keyword list; money math);
ch. 5 *is* the final exam — the soft-launch test grades the artifact against
ch. 1's promise. Ch. 5 has no separate Практика: the checklist is the practice.

## Maintenance notes

- **2026-07-05 retell (site shape):** all five chapters now acknowledge what
  the site became — **reviews are the core, the Compass courses the
  powerhouse** (owner's framing: the site began as her review site with
  occasional guides; he overtook it with mini-course ideas). Ch. 1 tells that
  story («Сайт, который мы задумали, — и сайт, который получился») and names
  the provenance rule; ch. 2 draws the reviews-vs-courses boundary; ch. 3
  places courses in the traffic plan (reviews get found, courses get
  remembered); ch. 4 calls the free courses the email magnet; ch. 5 gained an
  **unchecked** checklist item: every Compass course states its provenance at
  the top. The reader courses (homeopathy, herbalism) do NOT carry such a
  label yet — the founder course's ch. 1 is currently the site's only public
  disclosure that some courses are LLM-drafted; adding per-course labels is
  the open task that checklist item points at.

- Chapter order is driven by `chapter:` frontmatter, not `date` — dates keep
  the real authoring days (07-01…07-05) and must not be "fixed" to match order.
- When a roadmap milestone makes a chapter's claims lived experience, retell
  that chapter (both locales) and keep the Trail/Bridge weave intact.
- A sixth founder guide would mean renumbering deliberately — extend this plan
  first, per the blueprint's outline-before-writing rule.
